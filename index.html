Hi, my name is Jack Corrigan. I'm a research analyst at the Center for Security and Emerging Technology, where I write about the tech workforce, the U.S. innovation ecosystem, industrial policy, artificial intelligence and security issues.

Before coming to CSET, I reported on government technology policy for Nextgov and helped write a book about the growing social, political and economic power of multinational corporations.

You can find some of my published work here. If you'd like to get in touch, don't hesitate to reach out.
